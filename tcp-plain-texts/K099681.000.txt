Treasures and her God adore  BEHOLD ye FAIR how radiant Colours glow  What dyes the Rose what paints the Heav'nly Bow  The purpling Shade the rich refracted Ray  And all the bright Diversity of Day Shield of Nature and the Fence of Life  The ambient Atmosphere embracing all  The wide Circumf'rence of this circling Ball  The Vehicle of Life to those that breath  On solid Land or liquid Waves Mysterious Energy stupendous Theme  Immediate Mover of this boundless Frame  Who can thy Essence or thy Pow'r explain  The Sons of Wisdom seek thy Source in vain  Thyself invisible yet seen thy Laws
