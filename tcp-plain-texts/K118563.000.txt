Nor war tho' that is worse  But Ireland's sons endure alas  A selfinflicted Curse  Go where you will throughout the Realm  You'll find that every vice  In Cities Villages and Towns  From WHISKEY takes its rise  The Prince of darkness never sent  To Man a deadlier foe  "My name is Legion" it may say  The source of every woe  Nor does the fiend alone deprive  The labourer of his wealth  That is not
